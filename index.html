Hackathon?

A hackathon is a day long competition in which students work in teams to create an innovative solution to a problem of their choosing using all sorts of technologies. They also have the option to participate in mini-tournaments, workshops, and lectures throughout the course of the event. At the end of the day, students pitch their solutions to judges for prizes. The event is open to students of any skill-level and is a fun way to explore new interests and learn.

You can find us at the email royalbayhacks@gmail.com, with a Discord coming soon.

The event will be from 8:00AM - 10:30PM at Royal Bay Secondary School, with a date to come soon.

There isn't really a theme, the contestants will be working on software, hardware, middleware, and all other sorts of technical solutions to real-world problems people face.

Yes, there will be prizes available in two categories as well as for mini-events throughout the day.
